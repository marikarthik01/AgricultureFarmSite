ABOUT US


Praveena Agriculture Farm

Our garden is located at poolampatti. It is a small Village near palani in Oddanchatram Block at Dindigul District of Tamil Nadu State, India. It comes under marichilabhu Panchayath. It is located 56 KM towards west from District head quarters Dindigul. 16 KM from palani. 8 KM from Thoppampatti. Oddanchatram , Palani , Dindigul are the near by Cities to Poolampatti. Poolampatti is surrounded by Thoppampatti Block towards west , Palani Block towards south , Mulanur Block towards North..

  • We are aimed to provide a high quality range of vegetables and fruits in our farm.

  • We use only organic and bio manure for growing the guava tree and other vegetables.

  • We are Supplying Guava, Drumstick, Pumpkin and Tomato throughout the Tamilnadu.
